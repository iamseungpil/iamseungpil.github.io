Nice to meet you. I'm a passionate Master's student who embraces challenges in unfamiliar domains. Although my primary interest lies in the field of brain-inspired AI, I also have a broad curiosity that extends to other areas of artificial intelligence, including NLP and reinforcement learning. Additionally, I maintain a keen interest in interdisciplinary subjects such as cognitive science and STS.

I am currently solving visual reasoning benchmark, Abstraction and Reasoning Corpus (ARC) with Prof. Sundong Kim in GIST. ARC is a kind of IQ test for computers - if we can solve this challenge, we might be able to develop AI with genuine reasoning capabilities. Perhaps you'll soon meet an AI that surpasses human intelligence. You might find my name there!
